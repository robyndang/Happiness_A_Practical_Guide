ACTIVITY



This week, I’d like you to meditate for at least 10 minutes everyday. You don’t have to do anything special, just sit with your back straight, concentrate on your breathing, and clear your mind. This can be hard for beginners so I suggest following guided meditations which you can find on Youtube and Spotify. Netflix came out with a series called Headspace Guide To Meditation too where they teach you about meditation and also use the latter half of each episode to guide you through the process.
